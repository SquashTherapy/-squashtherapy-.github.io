About Us

We are dedicated to providing innovative ADHD therapy using the game of squash. Our unique approach helps children improve focus, enhance working memory, and develop better coordination.

Our Services

We offer specialized squash-based therapy sessions for children with ADHD and other concentration-related disorders. Our programs are tailored to each child's needs and focus on improving cognitive functions through engaging physical activity.

For Medical Professionals

Learn how to refer patients and discover the benefits of our program for your practice. We work closely with healthcare providers to ensure comprehensive care for children with ADHD.
